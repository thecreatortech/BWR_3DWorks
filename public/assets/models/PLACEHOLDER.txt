PLACEHOLDER - Download actual GLB files and replace this

The website is configured to load GLB files from this folder.
Until you add real GLB files, you may see "Loading..." or errors.

QUICK START:
1. Download FREE 3D model files from:
   - Sketchfab.com (download as GLB/GLTF)
   - Thingiverse.com (download STL, then convert to GLB)
   - Printables.com
   - CGTrader.com

2. Rename them to match the filenames in README.md

3. Replace placeholder files with real .glb files

Convert STL to GLB using:
- https://products.aspose.app/3d/conversion/stl-to-glb
- Blender (File > Export > glTF 2.0)

Example good searches:
- "minimalist vase"
- "geometric sculpture"  
- "parametric jewelry"
- "architectural model"
